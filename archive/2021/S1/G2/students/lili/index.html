Im Lili, welcome to my digital media project.

My connection to the ocean began at a young age. My Grandfather was a professional yachtsman and my Pop a fisherman. The sea has always been a rich part of my journey in life.

It has influences where I choose to live. Where I choose travel. If I am not near the ocean, I feel a sense of claustrophobia.

Recently I discovered that sand is made up of broken down shell matter and quartz crystal. Quartz is known to create balance and restore healing energy so when I spend time at the beach it brings me a sense of calmness. The ocean is my happy place.
