Yet more Reflection
11 June 2020

the following is a collection of words that I googled this semester to find their meaning. In almost every case I had an understanding of the word prior to searching it; however this understanding is not always one that fits the generally accepted meaning.

blog verb: add new material to or regularly update a blog.
conquer verb: overcome and take control of (a place or people) by military force. Relating to: defeat, beat, vanquish, trounce, annihilate
concur verb: be of the same opinion; agree. happen or occur at the same time; coincide.
contingent adjective: subject to chance. occurring or existing only if (certain circumstances) are the case; dependent on. Relating to: chance, accidental, fortuitous, possible, unforeseen, dependent, conditional.
contrived adjective: deliberately created rather than arising naturally or spontaneously. Relating to: forced, strained, studied, artificial, affected, put-on.
emersion noun: the process of emerging from water after being submerged. the reappearance of a celestial body after its eclipse or occultation.
empirical adjective: based on, concerned with, or verifiable by observation or experience rather than theory or pure logic. Related to: observed, seen, factual, actual, real, verifiable, first-hand.
immersion noun: deep mental involvement in something.
kibosh noun:put an end to. verb: dispose of decisively.
material noun: the matter from which a thing is or can be made. Relating to: matter, substance, stuff, medium. adjective: denoting or consisting of physical objects rather than the mind or spirit. Relating to physical, corporeal, tangible, non-spiritual, mundane, worldly. Maquette noun: a sculptor's small preliminary model or sketch.
melatonin noun: a hormone secreted by the pineal gland which inhibits melanin formation and is thought to be concerned with regulating the reproductive and sleep cycle.
ontology noun: the branch of metaphysics dealing with the nature of being. a set of concepts and categories in a subject area or domain that shows their properties and the relations between them.
pattern noun: a repeated design or a model used as a guide . Relating to design, decoration, motif, marking, ornament, ornamentation.
permeable adjective: (of a material or membrane) allowing liquids or gases to pass through it. Relating to: porous, pervious, penetrable, spongy, absorbent, absorptive.
plethora noun: a large or excessive amount of something.
psychedelic adjective: relating to or denoting drugs (especially LSD) that produce hallucinations and apparent expansion of consciousness. Relating to Pharmaceutics, Narcotics, Popular Music
ruthless adjective: having or showing no pity or compassion for others.
supersede verb: take the place of (a person or thing previously in authority or use); supplant. ruthless adjective: having or showing no pity or compassion for others.
tensegrity noun: the characteristic property of a stable three-dimensional structure consisting of members under tension that are contiguous and members under compression that are not.
transistor noun:a semiconductor device with three connections, capable of amplification in addition to rectification.
verdant adjective: (of countryside) green with grass or other rich vegetation. Related to: green, leafy, grassy, grass-covered, lush, rich, flourishing.
whimsical adjective: playfully quaint or fanciful, especially in an appealing and amusing way. acting or behaving in a capricious manner. Relating to: fanciful, playful, mischievous, waggish, quaint.
